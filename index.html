ABOUT ME

Welcome! The name is Tze Lun. Formerly a mechatronic engineering student at the University of Nottingham Malaysia. Throughout his bachelor's degree, he attained a wealth of practical and theoretical knowledge on robotics, industrial automation, electronics, CAD design, and control system. Despite that, his interest in software and algorithms deepened, which became the impetus to matriculate at the University of Tokyo for his master's in Japan. Being a scholar in a surgical robotics lab, he successfully devised a surgical bone drilling VR simulator, with the core of his research involving the integration of a realistic real time drilling audio generator based on deep learning.

Besides the tech-geeky persona, Tze Lun enjoys extracurricular activities. He used to be the event planner for IEEE Student Chapter at his alma mater. He participated in two business pitching events with a group of friends, working hand-in-hand to devise a business masterplan to present to a panel of experienced entrepreneurs. He also held many leadership titles during his high school years. Currently, his hobbies are cooking, fitness, and practising the electronic piano. Though he manages to interpret the music sheet, he is very much still an amateur on the piano. (sigh)
